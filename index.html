GBFS Viewer

A simple viewer for GBFS feeds.

This instance of GBFS Viewer is for Providence, Rhode Island. It shows realtime vehicle locations and station information from the JUMP Bike Share program, and realtime vehicle locations from current operators of the E-Scooter Share Program (if they provide GBFS Feeds).

Providence has designated five "zones" for the vehicle fleet distribution requirements in its E-Scooter Share Program. These five zones are shown in orange. Clicking on each zone will show a count of vehicles and stations within each.
